About us

Araksa Partnership

ARAKSA PARTNERSHIP Advocates, Receiver & Administrator For Bankruptcy is an Indonesian law firm dedicated and committed to providing excellence and precise legal advice and legal services. Established by professional Advocates who come from different backgrounds and expertise, who are united in one goal to provide comprehensive and effective services in the field of law for local and international clients.

Our extensive cooperation network such as China, Korea, Japan, Singapore, and other countries provides a sense of security for clients to face legal problems, especially in cases arising from cross-border transactions.

With the competence and experiences gained by our lawyers either in the private or public sector, we believe we can understand the diverse needs of each client and make the best strategy out of it. Through its work, the firm has been keen on seeking truth and justice. Thus, it would contribute more to the nation and the global world.
